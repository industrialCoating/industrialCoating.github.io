ABOUT US

In 1985, Mahmoud Merzi started Merzi Painting and Construction as a Painting Contractor. In 1999, he added a B-license for general building contractor and never looked back. Industrial Coating & Restoration Inc. (ICR) was formed in 2002 under License number 880502. We are 100% solid material applicator.

Operating since 2002 under License number 880502, ICR has undertaken large projects ranging from $250,000 to $3,000,000. Our company's expertise and knowledge is prevelant in our work and numerous references. We currently hold the following contractor licenses:

  • A- GENERAL ENGINEERING
  • B- GENERAL BUILDING
  • C33- PAINTING AND DECORATING
  • D6- CONCRETE
  • D12- SYNTHETIC PRODUCTS
  • D38- SAND AND WATER BLASTING
