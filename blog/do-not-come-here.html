We Aren't Your Traditional Soccer Club. DON'T BRING YOUR KID HERE ⚠️

By Anees Merzi | April 2025

If you’re looking for a traditional soccer club, let us save you some time: don’t bring your kid here.

We aren’t in this for trophies, clout, or status. We’re not here to make you feel good about buying fancy gear or collecting participation medals.

We are here to build kids that thrive on and off the field and we’ll do whatever it takes to get them there.

We Built Our Own System Because the Current One is Broken

Most clubs run the same tired playbook. We burned ours a long time ago.

Our model is unorthodox, intentional, and built for real growth. Four programs. All connected. All focused on developing athletes and leaders, not just soccer players.

  • Life Skills Training — Financial literacy, mentorship, online safety, peer pressure resistance. What other club teaches this? None that we’ve seen. Prove us wrong.
  • Street Soccer League — Organized pickup games. Minimal coaching. Maximum creativity. Kids learn how to play and not just how to follow instructions.
  • Community Fitness — Speed, strength, agility, footwork. Skills that private trainers charge for are taught for free every week.
  • Competitive Soccer — Earn your spot. No politics. No shortcuts. If you commit and put in the work, we’ll get you ready to compete at the next level.

This Isn’t for Everyone — And That’s the Point

We practice on concrete, dirt, and grass. No lights? We bring our own. No fancy fields? Doesn’t matter. We train anywhere, anytime, with whoever shows up ready to work.

Our pilot team didn’t come from elite travel programs. But in one year, they’ve gone from brand new to battling established clubs and winning.

Imagine what we can do with more time, more kids, and more families who buy into this vision.

We’re Proudly Unorthodox

Here’s what makes us different (we’re not changing for anyone):

  • Swearing-In Ceremony: Because joining 703 Warriors means something.
  • The Haka: Because passion and unity matter here.
  • Football-Inspired Coaching: We coach hard because we care.
  • Zero Tolerance for Entitlement or Disrespect: If that’s what you’re about, stay home.

We develop teams, not rosters. We raise leaders, not followers. And we build grateful, tough, focused athletes who can succeed far beyond soccer.

Choose Your Path, But Choose Honestly

If you want real development, mental, physical, emotional, we’re here for you.

If you want easy, good luck somewhere else.

703 Warriors isn’t trying to fit in. We’re building something different. And different works if you do.
