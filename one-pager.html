The Challenge

Talent is being sidelined!

Competitive youth soccer clubs cost $3,000 per year and often hold training at 5pm. For many families, that is unreasonable and results in fewer safe, structured environments for kids during critical developmental years.

We fill this need and want to deploy our model to other area codes in the future.
