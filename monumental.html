703 Warriors Soccer

Who We Are

703 Warriors is a community-based travel soccer and mentorship program, serving primarily lower-income children at no cost to them. With high-quality volunteer training sessions three times a week, the program emphasizes fitness, inclusiveness, and teamwork. In just 18 months, our competitive teams are not only competing with pay-to-play programs but advancing to final rounds and winning! The demand is growing and support is needed to sustain the program and create new opportunities for girls.

Community Impact

Building Leaders On and Off the Field

The 703 Warriors program is a game-changing solution in youth sports. We're building a repeatable, community-powered model where cost never benches talent, creating a blueprint for equitable youth sports that could be deployed in communities across the country. This mission is at the heart of what we do, and with your help, we can amplify our impact and provide life-changing opportunities for young athletes across the DMV.

The Opportunity

Why This Is Needed Now

For many girls, especially girls of color, sports are a critical pathway to developing confidence and leadership skills that prepare them for future success. Our program is expanding, intentionally designed to foster these characteristics.

  • >
    Developing Leaders: Create an environment where girls feel empowered to lead on the field. A skill that translates directly to their schools and communities as they grow.
  • >
    Building Confidence: Resilience, teamwork, and self-belief! Equip them with the tools to take on leadership roles as adults.
  • >
    Intentional Coaching: Coaches selected and trained to be mentors who foster character, confidence, and leadership.
