A Practical Giving Opportunity

Local. Tangible. Easy to Explain.

Many clients want to use Donor Advised Fund dollars in a way that feels close to home and easy to understand. 703 Warriors offers exactly that: direct support for youth who would otherwise face financial and structural barriers to sports, mentorship, and healthy development.

  • ✓
    Local impact: Programs serve low-income, immigrant, and underserved youth across Arlington, Falls Church, and Alexandria.
  • ✓
    Clear outcomes: Clients can understand exactly what their contribution supports.
  • ✓
    High efficiency: A volunteer-powered model stretches each charitable dollar further.
